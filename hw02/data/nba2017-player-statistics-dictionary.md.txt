# Statistics for NBA Players in 2017
"This data contains information about basketball players, including their name, team, position, salary, and points scored, among other variables."
'Source: www.basketball-reference.com'
'Team link (Golden State Warriors) = http://www.basketball-reference.com/teams/GSW/2017.html'

This data has 441 rows and 24 columns.

Column names:

- 'Player': first and last names of player
- 'Team': 3-letter team abbreviation
- 'Position': player's position
- 'Experience': years of experience in NBA
- 'Salary': player salary in dollars
- 'Rank': rank of player in his team
- 'Age': age of player at the start of Fevruary first of the 2017 season
- 'GP': games played during regular season
- 'GS': games started
- 'MIN': minutes played during regular season
- 'FGM': field goals made
- 'FGA': field goal attempts
- 'Points3': 3-point field goals
- 'Points3_atts': 3-points field goal attempts
- 'Points2': 2-point field goals
- 'Points2_atts': 2-point field goal attempts
- 'FTM': free throws made
- 'FTA': free throw attempts
- 'OREB': offensive rebounds
- 'DREB': defenseive rebounds
- 'AST': assists
- 'STL': steals
- 'BLK': blocks
- 'TO': turnovers
