# Data dictionary for NBA player statistics.

Main source: `www.basketball-reference.com`.
This data includes various statistics for NBA players displayed in alphabetical order of the first name.
The data includes:

- Games played
- Minutes
- Field goals made
- Field goals attempted
- Field goal percentage
- Points 3 made
- Points 3 attempted
- Points 3 percentage
- Points 2 made
- Points 2 attempted
- Points 2 percentage
- Points 1 made
- Points 1 attempted
- Points 1 percentage
- Offensive rebounds
- Defensive rebounds
- Assists
- Steals
- Blocks
- Turnovers
- Fouls
